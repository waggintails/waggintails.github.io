Questions Families Ask

Do you offer dog day care?

Yes. Day care is the heart of Waggin' Tails. Call (269) 579-3201 to ask about the daily routine and current openings.

Do you offer overnight boarding?

Yes, boarding is part of what Waggin' Tails offers. Call to ask about availability and what your dog should bring for an overnight stay.

Where are you located?

10868 Cressey Rd in Plainwell, Michigan, serving dog families around Richland, Plainwell, and the Gull Lake area.

How much does day care or boarding cost?

Rates depend on the care your dog needs. Call for current pricing and availability. The team will give you a straight answer.

What does my dog need before a first visit?

Every dog is different, so the team walks new families through first-visit requirements by phone. Call ahead and they will help you get set up.
